# Charter

CUMT Alethic Insight Lab is a student-led AI society-lab and research-engineering community at China University of Mining and Technology.

It serves three roles:

1. a CUMT student AI club;
2. a CUMT AI society-lab;
3. a student-led AI research and engineering community.

## Mission

The lab helps undergraduate students learn AI through research reading, engineering practice, open-source collaboration, reproducible experiments, and responsible use of AI tools.

## Scope

Primary directions include language and NLP, vision and multimodal learning, agents and reinforcement learning, trustworthy / alethic intelligence, AI infrastructure, open-source practice, and research-engineering training.

## Public Identity

The lab should be described as student-led unless explicit authorization allows a stronger institutional claim.

## Operating Principles

- Keep work evidence-based and reproducible where practical.
- Prefer clear documentation and maintainable systems.
- Use Pull Requests for non-trivial changes.
- Protect sensitive materials.
- Prepare future students to inherit the infrastructure safely.
